--- layout: default title: Link your Discord account. description: A simple guide to learn how to link your Discord account to your Hypixel profile in the InfiniteVoid Discord. ---

Link your Discord account.

  1. Join the InfiniteVoid Discord Server.
  2. Join the Hypixel Network.
  3. Open your profile menu by right-clicking the player head in your hotbar.
  4. Navigate to the Social Media editor.
  5. Left click the Discord button.
  6. Enter your Discord name and discriminator [example: User#0001] in your chat window and press enter.
  7. Navigate to the #bot-commands channel in the Discord server.
  8. Use the !link <minecraft name> command to link your account.
  9. Congratulations, you should have now linked your account!
